### About

I am a Cloud & Systems Engineer and Master's student in Advanced Information Systems. I design, deploy, and operate infrastructure across on-premises and cloud environments, with a strong interest in Linux, Kubernetes, virtualization, distributed storage, observability, automation, and security.

My current focus is **platform engineering**: building secure, reproducible, and self-service infrastructure that makes complex systems easier to use. I am also exploring **sovereign AI infrastructure**, including self-hosted inference, GPU workload orchestration, portable LLM serving, and auditable agentic systems that can run without proprietary cloud dependencies.

Beyond engineering, I enjoy turning complex technical subjects into open-source projects, articles, workshops, and educational platforms.
